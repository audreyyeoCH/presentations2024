Ten rules to make conferences more accessible (Joo et al 2022):

  1. Set a vision for DEI
  2. Create a safe and welcoming environment
  3. Gather an inclusive and diverse organizing team
  4. Consciously counteract bias in the conference program
  5. Design a strong online component
  6. Making the conference accessible to people with disabilities
  7. Make room for the linguistic diversity
  8. Build an inclusive communication strategy
  9. Allocate adequate financial resources to support inclusion goals
  10. Make the conference part of a long-term process for inclusion
